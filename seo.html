SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It is the process of optimizing a website to rank higher in search engine results pages (SERPs). SEO can be done on-page or off-page. On-page SEO refers to optimizing the content and code of a website to make it more search engine friendly. Off-page SEO refers to building backlinks to a website from other websites.

Search engines use a variety of factors to rank websites in SERPs. These factors include the quality and relevance of the content, the number and quality of backlinks, the website's loading speed, and the website's mobile-friendliness. SEO can help improve a website's ranking in SERPs by optimizing it for these factors.
