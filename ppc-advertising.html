F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions



PPC stands for pay-per-click. It is a type of online advertising where advertisers pay a fee each time someone clicks on their ad. PPC ads are displayed on search engine results pages (SERPs) and other websites.

When someone searches for a keyword or phrase on a search engine, the search engine will display a list of relevant ads. The ads are ranked based on a number of factors, including the advertiser's bid, the quality of the ad, and the relevance of the ad to the search query.

PPC advertising can be a very effective way to reach your target audience and generate leads and sales. Some of the benefits of PPC advertising include: Targeted reach, Quick results, Measurable results.

The cost of PPC advertising varies depending on a number of factors, including the keywords you choose, the competition for those keywords, and the quality of your ad. However, PPC advertising can be very cost-effective, especially when compared to other forms of advertising.
